# 风格 DNA

## One Sentence

Clean white background, warm hand-drawn article sketch, friendly chibi IP character, sparse Chinese handwritten labels, simple visual metaphor, clear structure without PPT feeling.

## Required

- 16:9 horizontal article illustration.
- Clean white background.
- Hand-drawn pencil/crayon texture, with slightly rough outlines and visible sketch strokes.
- The IP boy appears as the core action subject.
- Large whitespace: main subject around 40%-60% of canvas; at least 30%-35% blank area.
- Sparse Chinese handwritten labels: at most 5-8 labels, each ideally 2-8 Chinese characters.
- One image explains one core action, structure, state, or metaphor.
- Natural scene-based structure; do not write the structure type on the image.

## Character Palette

- Black/dark brown: hair, outlines, T-shirt, main sketch lines.
- Peach: skin and soft blush.
- Charcoal/dark gray: pants and secondary shapes.
- Bright blue: sneakers and occasional accent when useful.
- Red/orange/blue: sparse annotation accents only.

## Annotation Colors

- Black: main line art, object labels, structure labels.
- Orange: main path, arrows, movement, handoff, flow.
- Red: warnings, bottlenecks, problems, key reminders.
- Blue: secondary notes, system feedback, supporting context; also echoes the character's sneakers.

Use colors sparingly. The image should still feel like a white-page sketch.

## Avoid

- Do not make a PPT infographic.
- Do not make a formal flowchart.
- Do not make a commercial vector illustration.
- Do not make a glossy anime poster.
- Do not make a children's sticker sheet.
- Do not use complex background, gradient, shadow, paper texture, or realistic UI screenshots.
- Do not explain every node.
- Do not add top-left titles like "Workflow", "系统架构图", "路线图", or "常见坑".

## Aesthetic Direction

Warm, clean, thoughtful, slightly playful, creator-like, useful, and memorable.

Avoid cold corporate diagrams, over-cute mascot art, overly dense teaching slides, or exact copies of the reference standing pose.
